On a whisker and a prayer - my debut book!

A moving, uplifting true story of a girl who loves animals.

After qualifying as a Graphic Designer at University, she must then relocate to start a new job as a packaging designer at a chocolate factory. She is faced with a number of set backs, along with health issues, but as it turns out, being made redundant was to be a blessing in disguise!

A tale of ups and downs, laughter and tears, redundancy, health issues and the amazing power of pets!

My name is Lisa and I have been an animal lover since I was able to talk! I have always had pets and understand how important they are to us - however big or small! They are key members of any family and bring so much love and enjoyment!

Since the age of 11 I have devoted my free time to being with animals; whether it has been helping care for and look after horses or cleaning out cats in my local cat rescues, I try to surround myself with animals all the time!

I can promise you that I will look after your pet as if it were one of my own - and everyone who knows me will tell you, I worship the ground those little paws walk on! Rest assured, while you are away, your pet will be in VERY SAFE HANDS!
